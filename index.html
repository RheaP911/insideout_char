JOY

As her name states, Joy is the very essence of happiness, perkiness, warm feelings and positive feelings in general, as such, she loves to look on the bright side of things. She is by far the most optimistic, confident, peppy, determined and loving out of all of the emotions, wishing and wanting nothing but the best out of everyone and everything.

SADNESS

As her name suggests, Sadness embodies sorrow, gloom, and hurt feelings. She is highly sensitive and prone to tears. Despite being slow, she observes and reads a lot. Sadness possesses common sense and pragmatism, which proved valuable during Riley’s mind journey.

FEAR

Fear, as his name states, is the literal embodiment of primal feelings of being scared and fight-or-flight. He cares very much about Riley's safety and her well-being and is very concerned about the choices Riley makes in her life. Unlike the rest of the Emotions he is wary, jumpy, and suspicious.
